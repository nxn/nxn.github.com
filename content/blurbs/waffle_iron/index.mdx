Waffle-Iron is a sudoku puzzle solver and generator written in Rust. It's capable of being compiled into WebAssembly and
executed from within a browser, where it offers consistent and improved performance in comparison to similar JavaScript 
implementations.

Project Repository: [GitHub](https://github.com/nxn/waffle-iron)
